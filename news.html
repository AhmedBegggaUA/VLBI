José Manuel Ferrándiz, new President of the Scientific Panel of GGOS.

We congratulate our distinguished colleague, José Manuel Ferrándiz, for his recent election as President of the Scientific Panel of GGOS - Global Geodetic Observing System #GGOS.

This achievement constitutes a clear recognition of the scientific capacity and leadership of Professor Jose M. Ferrandiz in the field of #Geodesy. It also reflects the commitment and continuous support of the University of Alicante to its initiatives, which are increasingly gaining greater international projection.

We wish him success in his new responsibilities, he has the full support from the Geodesy Research Group of Alicante University in this exciting stage of his career.

05/12/2023

LUCIA DANIELA DEL NIDO HERRANZ, new team member of UAVAC

With this new incorporation UAVAC reinforces its research area to improve the VLBI analysis and EOP predictions making use of novel theories, updated models and advanced mathematics algorithms (e.g. machine learning). Welcome LUCIA!

16/10/2023

Marcin Ligas, our newest guest researcher

We are happy to welcome our newest guest researcher Marcin Ligas from Integrated Geodesy and Cartography Department of University of Science and Technology in Kraków (Poland). During these weeks, Marcin will investigate the Earth Orientation Parameters (EOP)prediction making use of advanced mathematical algorithms.

16/10/2023
